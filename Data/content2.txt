## Master Level
The “Master” badge is the highest designated ranking on Ratio. Masters are the best of the best and they have data-backed results that place them in the top 25% of a given metric in an industry. 
### Ratio Masters
+	Delivers outstanding campaign performance consistently
+	Possess the most extensive body of work on the platform and years of experience
+	Have consistently demonstrated the top 25% of results in their category
