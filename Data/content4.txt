## Professional Level
The “Professional” badge designates performance that is below or meets national averages for that category. They perform in the bottom 50% of a metric in any given industry. Professionals have a solid body of work that demonstrates their expertise in any given category, and they have run multiple successful campaigns in any given industry.
### Ratio Professionals
-	A larger body of work to sample from contributors, but not as much as an expert 
-	Upload several campaigns in industry segments that show consistently good results
-	Have consistently demonstrated results in the bottom 50% or in line with national averages in any given category
